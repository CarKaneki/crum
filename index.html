Basic Cyber Security

The World wide web can be a very helpful space as well as a dangerous one following these tips can keep you and your personal information safe from hackers and unwanted eyes.

the most important thing first to check is to make sure that your firewall is on for both public and private networks. Another aspect that is sometimes overlooked is making sure your operating system is up to date with its most recent software update avaivable.
